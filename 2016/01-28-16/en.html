International Health Alert : ZIKA Virus

January 28, 2016

Due to the outbreak of the Zika Virus, health authorities recommend that pregnant women avoid travel to those countries in Latin America and the Caribbean affected by this virus, Copa Airlines will assist those pregnant woman that contact the airline, providing flexibility in the reschedule of their trip.

Health authorities have recommended pregnant women do not visit the following regions and countries affected by the ZIKA Virus:

  • Caribbean
  • Central America
  • Mexico
  • Puerto Rico
  • South America

All pregnant women and their traveling companions, who were scheduled to travel to the regions and countries mentioned above, will receive assistance with the following information:
