Copa Airlines announces nonstop flights to Holguin, Cuba, the Airline’s third Cuban Destination.

Copa Airlines will begin direct flights to Holguin, Cuba, from the Hub of the Americas in Panama on June 21, 2016. Holguin is Copa’s third Cuban destination

Holguin’s numerous tourist attractions make it one of Cuba’s most important cities

After more than 20 years of operations to Cuba, Copa Airlines service now includes seven daily flights to Havana and since 2014, four weekly flights to Santa Clara

Panama City, Panama, Jan. 25, 2016.

Copa Airlines {NYSE: CPA}, a subsidiary of Copa Holdings, S.A. and member of the Star Alliance global airline network, has announced the addition of Holguin, Cuba, to its route network. Copa will begin nonstop flights two times a week between Holguin and Panama City, Panama, as of June 21, 2016. The addition of Holguin strengthens Copa’s leadership in the region, airline officials say.

“We are pleased to announce Holguín as our third destination in Cuba, which will enhance connectivity between that country and the rest of the continent, and contribute to the development of tourism and trade opportunities,” said Pedro Heilbron, CEO of Copa Airlines. “We are committed to expanding connectivity to Cuba, helping raise its profile as a tourism and cultural epicenter of the region.”

Considered the third-most important tourist destination on the island, Holguin offers visitors a unique travel experience featuring more than 40 beaches with crystal-clear waters, a rich marine life and forests.

Culture and history tourism aficionados will enjoy visiting the Cuchillas del Toa Biosphere Reserve and the Alexander von Humboldt National Park, both classified as UNESCO World Heritage Sites. In 1492, Christopher Columbus proclaimed the area “the most beautiful land that human eyes have ever seen.”

Copa will begin operations with two flights weekly, on Tuesdays and Saturdays. Flight CM 400 will depart at 9:01 a.m. from Tocumen International Airport in Panama, arriving at Frank País International Airport in Holguin at 12:31 p.m. The return flight, CM 401, will depart Holguín at 1:21 p.m., arriving in Panama at 2:47 p.m.

Copa Airlines has timed the flight for rapid, convenient connections at the Hub of the Americas in Panama to Santiago, Chile; Buenos Aires and Cordoba, Argentina; Quito and Guayaquil, Ecuador; Montevideo, Uruguay; Lima, Peru; San Jose, Costa Rica; Bogotá, Colombia; Sao Pablo, Brazil; Mexico City and Cancun, México; Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic; Caracas, Venezuela; and Santa Cruz de la Sierra, Bolivia.

Copa Airlines flies to 73 destinations in 31 countries in North, Central and South America and the Caribbean through its Hub of the Americas in Panama, which offers more international connections than any other hub in Latin America. For the past three consecutive years, FlightStats has recognized Copa Airlines as the most on-time airline in Latin America. In addition, in January 2016, OAG ranked Copa Airlines’ on-time performance No. 1 in Latin America and No. 2 among airlines throughout the world.
